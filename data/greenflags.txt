[GF-01] Verified Corporate Identity
Description:
Employer domain, company name, and job title align clearly with legitimate sources.
Evidence:
- Email ends with verified company domain (@microsoft.com, @kroger.com).
- Company website and location match job description.
- Employer has active LinkedIn or Glassdoor profile.

[GF-02] Structured, Complete Job Details
Description:
Legitimate posts specify clear roles, responsibilities, and qualifications.
Evidence:
- Headings like “Responsibilities”, “Qualifications”, “Benefits”.
- Tasks use professional verbs (“Design”, “Implement”, “Analyze”).
- Mentions department or supervisor.

[GF-03] Realistic Compensation & Requirements
Description:
Pay range and experience expectations are coherent with market norms.
Evidence:
- Salary aligns with industry and region.
- Requirements match pay level and seniority.
- No “$120/hr no experience” type claims.

[GF-04] Professional Communication Channel
Description:
Official, traceable contact and application methods are used.
Evidence:
- Applications through ATS (Workday, Lever, Greenhouse).
- Recruiter email on company domain or verified LinkedIn.
- No off-platform screening (Telegram, WhatsApp).

[GF-05] Traceable Company Presence
Description:
Company has verifiable, active digital footprint.
Evidence:
- Career page on company website.
- Consistent listings on LinkedIn or Indeed.
- Public news or legal registration confirms existence.

[GF-06] Reasonable Hiring Timeline
Description:
Hiring process includes clear stages and expected duration.
Evidence:
- Mentions interview process and deadlines.
- No instant-hire or same-day offers.
- States review or background check steps.

[GF-07] Genuine Job Description Language
Description:
Text reads professionally with realistic tone and context.
Evidence:
- Mentions specific tools or tech stack.
- Balanced phrasing, no hyperbolic promotion.
- Clean grammar and formatting.

[GF-08] Employee-Centric Benefits
Description:
Standard benefits consistent with industry practices.
Evidence:
- Mentions healthcare, PTO, retirement plan, relocation.
- Clearly written benefits section.
- No unrealistic perks.

[GF-09] Legal or Ethical Disclosure
Description:
Includes compliance or diversity statements.
Evidence:
- “We are an equal opportunity employer…” clause.
- Mentions work authorization or legal requirements.

[GF-10] Consistency Across Platforms
Description:
Job details align across verified listings.
Evidence:
- Same job title and salary on multiple sites.
- Recruiter contact consistent everywhere.

[GF-11] Employer Branding Elements
Description:
Company culture and branding visible.
Evidence:
- Logo or colors consistent with corporate identity.
- Mentions mission, values, or diversity goals.

[GF-12] Secure & Transparent Application Process
Description:
Applications use secure portals and disclose data handling.
Evidence:
- HTTPS form links with privacy statement.
- No early personal data requests.

Usage Notes:
- Retrieve 3–5 green flags for Legit or Uncertain cases to justify verdict.
- Combine with Red Flags: if red > green by 3+ points, output “Fake”.
- Include GF codes and short evidence snippets in RAG output.
